## Overview

Created for my senior capstone, this project reimagines Pachisi, an ancient Indian race game, through a culturally grounded design system that restores a sense of cultural presence through design.

## Process

The goal was to develop a graphic design system inspired by mid-20th-century design, Indian vernacular graphics, print culture, and traditional motifs through the lens of modern structure and communicate it through a rulebook (1/2) and a physical artifact (2/2).
