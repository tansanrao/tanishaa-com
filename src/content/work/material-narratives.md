Material Narratives was built from a library of scans, paper textures, and repeated marks that could be abstracted into a visual identity. The goal was not to imitate tactility literally, but to suggest it through pacing, layering, and restrained contrast.

The process moved between physical sampling and digital composition. Tests looked at how texture survived reduction, which marks could become signatures, and how much irregularity the system could hold before consistency broke down.

The final identity balances softness with precision, creating a brand world that feels authored and tactile without becoming nostalgic.
