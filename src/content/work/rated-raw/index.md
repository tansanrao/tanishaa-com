## Overview

Rated Raw is a fictional production company and streaming platform centered around adult animation. Created for a motion graphics class, the project uses hand-drawn Procreate illustrations compiled, animated, and edited in Adobe After Effects to form the final sizzle reel.

Audio featured in this reel is copyrighted material owned by its respective creators and is used solely for non-commercial educational purposes.

## Process

The concept for Rated Raw was present from the beginning, but the visual direction and animation style developed through experimentation. Most of the drawings were created during a few sleepless nights, when the visual language and motion approach came together.
