## Overview

Crafted entirely from a single block of poplar wood, this hand-carved ashtray combines natural material with functional design. The removable lid is thoughtfully designed to serve as a standalone ashtray. Finished with a custom stain to attain a rustic look while still allowing the natural grain to show through.
